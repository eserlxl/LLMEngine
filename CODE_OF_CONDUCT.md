## Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

### Our Pledge
We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community.

### Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

### Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Contact: open an issue marked "coc" or email the maintainers if provided in project metadata.

For the full text of the Contributor Covenant, see `https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/`.
